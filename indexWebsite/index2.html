DIM SUM

Dim Sum is a traditional Cantonese food in Hong Kong. There are lots of local Dim Sum you can eat, for example, shrimp dumpling, xiao long bao, shaomai etc. If you want to have an experience like a local people, you must go to yum chai in London restaurant.

HOW to get there?

From MTR Mong Kok Station Exit E2, walk for about 3 minutes, you can find the Good Hope Bldg.

EGG WAFFLES

The most commonly mentioned street food is to be egg waffles. Egg waffles are more like foreign waffles and puffs. They can generally be translated as egg puffs or egg waffle. Egg waffle can also refer to checkered pie. The North Egg Puffs is very famous shop for eating the egg puffs

How to get there?

From MTR Jordan Station Exit D, walk for about 2 minutes.

CART NOODLES

Cart noodles is a popular local noodles in Hong Kong. You can choose the local snacks in your noodles like fishball, beef offal, tea eggs etc. We recommend you to go to Man Kee Cart Noodle in Sham Shui Po because it is famous in local people and the price is not high.

How to get there?

From Exit D of Sham Shui Po MTR station , walk 5-10 minute to Fuk Wing Street.

CHICKEN POT

Hot pot is a chinese food in China and chicken pot is a famous pot in Hong Kong. You can add everything in the pot like beef, vegetables etc. You can find Supreme restaurants in MongKok.

How to get there?

From MTR Mongkok station Exit E1, walk for about 5 minutes. You will find Chong Hing Square and go to 16 floor.

STEAMED RICE ROLL

Steamed rice roll is one of the breakfast choices for some Hong Kong people. They would like to put some sauce in the steamed rice roll like peanut sauce, chili sauce etc. Tong Da restaurant also have steamed rice roll and people will go there to eat the steamed rice roll in a far place. 

How to get there?

From MTR Yau Ma Tei Station Exit A2, walk for about 1 minute.
